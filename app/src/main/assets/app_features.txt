CAMPUS NETWORK APP NAVIGATION GUIDE
====================================

HOW TO CHECK CROWD STATUS:
Step 1: Tap "Campus" button on main page
Step 2: Tap "Live Crowd" 
Step 3: View crowd status for Canteen, Library, Gym, or Labs

HOW TO ORDER FOOD:
Step 1: Tap "Campus" button on main page
Step 2: Tap "Order Food"
Step 3: Browse menu and select items
Step 4: Place order

HOW TO SEND FEEDBACK:
Step 1: Tap "Campus" button on main page
Step 2: Tap "Send Feedback"
Step 3: Choose "Submit New Feedback"
Step 4: Select category (Library/Canteen/Hygiene/Other)
Step 5: Write feedback and submit

HOW TO VIEW CAMPUS NOTICES:
Step 1: Tap "Campus" button on main page
Step 2: Tap "Notices"
Step 3: Read campus-specific announcements

HOW TO VIEW ANNOUNCEMENTS:
Step 1: Tap "Announcements" button on main page
Step 2: Browse all announcements
Step 3: Filter by category if needed

HOW TO CHECK ACADEMIC CALENDAR:
Step 1: Tap "Calendar" button on main page
Step 2: Browse months using arrow buttons
Step 3: Tap on date to view event details

HOW TO ACCESS CLASSROOM FEATURES:
Step 1: Tap "Classroom" button on main page
Step 2: Choose:
   - "Attendance" to view attendance calendar (green=present, red=absent)
   - "Materials" to access study PDFs (Chemistry, Math, Previous Papers)
   - "Communication" to chat with teachers and classmates
   - "Timetable" to view weekly schedule

HOW TO VIEW BORROWED BOOKS:
Step 1: Tap "Personal Info" button on main page
Step 2: Expand "Library Details" section
Step 3: Tap "Show More" to see all borrowed books

HOW TO CHECK PAYMENT STATUS:
Step 1: Tap "Personal Info" button on main page
Step 2: Expand "Payment Details" section
Step 3: View all pending and past payments

HOW TO VIEW TIMETABLE (Guest):
Step 1: Login as Guest
Step 2: Tap "Timetable" button
Step 3: Pinch to zoom, drag to pan

HOW TO LOGOUT:
Tap the logout button (exit icon) in top-right corner

MAIN BUTTONS ON HOME:
- Classroom (attendance, materials, communication, timetable)
- Announcements (campus announcements)
- Social (coming soon)
- Campus (food, notices, feedback, crowd check)
- Calendar (academic events)
- Personal Info (profile, library, payments)

CAMPUS HUB FEATURES:
- Order Food - Pre-order from canteen
- Notices - Campus-specific announcements
- Send Feedback - Report issues about Library/Canteen/Hygiene/Other
- Live Crowd - Check crowd at Canteen/Library/Gym/Labs
